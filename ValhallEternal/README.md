# ValhallEternal
Upon building for release, this README.md is copied into the `Package` folder for thunderstore packaging. Remember to also edit the manifest.json and supply your own mod icon.

## Features

## Gefjun | Meadows

### Boons
- Bounty of the lands | Increases yield from harvests (chance?)
- Lineage of legend | Increases amount of gold or valuables found (chance?)
- Prosperity | Fishing is less stamina consuming?

## Vör | Blackforest

### Boons
- Ever Watchful | Recieve less damage from backstabs
- Wisdom of the Ages | Recieve bonus XP (chance? random?)
- Strength to Endure | provides a burst of stamina when close to death

## Skaði | Mountains

### Boons
- Eternal Huntress | Summons a wolf to aid you in combat, levels increase power, duration, and frequency
- Swift is the Shadow | Increases speed at night or on mountains?
- Vegence is mine | Poisons the attacker (% of damage taken?)

## Syn | Plains

### Boons
- Strength of the Valkyries | Percentage of highest weapon skill is added to all weapon skill checks
- Refusal of the Wnworthy | Prevents use of bows/crossbows, melee weapons deal +% dmg
- Guardian of the Gates | Tower shields block is %+ stronger

## Freya | Mistlands

### Boons
- Seiðr of Plenty | Increases base Eitr/eitr regen
- Perfect Form | Triggers a critical attack every x attacks
- Chosen of Freya | Prevents damage taken once every x minutes/hits?

## Hel | Ashlands

### Boons
- Hellfire Adaptation | Increases how long you can withstand incredible heat
- Balance of duality | Recieve Eitr/stamina when taking damage
- Time comes for all | Deal more damage when low health, recieve more damage
